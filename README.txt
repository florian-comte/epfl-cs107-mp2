This is a mini-game project made by two students (Florian COMTE and Nadim SABA) during our first semester at EPFL (for the course 'Programmation introduction').

----------------------{_HOW_TO_LAUNCH_}--------------------------

    To launch the game, run the Play.java file in our project

--------------------------{_AREAS_}------------------------------


    > Main menu :
        
        You choose between three gamemodes in the main menu :

        - Player vs Player : press A
        - Player vs AI : press B
        - Betting mode (AI vs AI) : press C


    > Theme selection :

        - If you want to play in a classic military theme, press A
        - If you like cats and strange unidentified creatures, press B


    > Betting mode, team selection :

        If you chose the betting mode, you will have to bet on either the blue team (A) or the Red team (B)
        Note : in the betting mode, initial positions of the units and the starting team are random.


    > Pause screen :

        - To mute/unmute the background music, press M
        - To turn on/off the button sounds, press S
        - To quit the game, press N
        - To resume the game, press SPACE


----------------------{_DURING_THE_GAME_}------------------------------------


    During the game, you can move the cursor up, down, right and left with the corresponding arrow in your keyboard

    > Select the unit you want to move by pressing ENTER and move the cursor in the position you want to move the unit to (in its movement radius) the press ENTER again to move it.

    > After you move the unit, a window will appear on the top right of your screen where the different actions that the unit can perform will be listed. Follow the instructions on this      
      screen to select the action you want to perform.

    > If you choose the action attack, you can change the unit you want to attack with the left and right arrows, and once you are ready to attack, press ENTER.

    > If you are done with your turn, press TAB to switch to the next player.

    > Any time during the game, you can pause by pressing SPACE.

    > To switch to the next level, you can press N anytime.

    > To reset, press R.

    > If you chose the military theme, the winning team will perform a celebration dance. If you get bored in the middle of it, you can skip it by pressing ENTER.
